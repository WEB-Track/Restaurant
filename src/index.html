Executive Chef

Executive Chef Michael Taff opened King Moon in March 2014. As an Executive Chef decided to pursue his dream of opening an Asian Restaurant and Catering business.

Chef Taff has a vast culinary background and a is a graduate of Culinary Institute of America (CIA) in Texas. He began his career as a teenager dishwasher at the Red’s in Harry, TX. He quick worked his way up head cook and excelled from there.

His culinary path has included time at House of Steaks in Dallas, TX where he worked as an Executive Chef, and eventually went on to become owner and Executive Chef of King Moon in Dallas, TX. Prior to opening King Moon, Chef Taff spend he last 15 years working in places like John Miller Corp. where he began as a Sous Chef. After a few months there he opened one of their new restaurants as the Executive Chef. Later he worked as an Executive Chef for Tommy Wright Hotel Corp. where he helped them to open multiple hotel restaurants. He was name “Chef of The Year” in 2014 by Fort Worth Restaurant News.

Chef Taff enjoys the outdoors, and activities like swimming, riding bicycles, hiking, fishing, riding motorcycles and running. He currently is live with his wife and two dogs in Lewisville, TX.
